Life On Land

The world is currently facing problems such as extreme deforestation, land degradation, threats to biodiversity, impact on livelihoods, and climate change. The SDG Life on Land is significant to combat these challenges that affect the state of the earth. Through this, terrestrial ecosystems are protected and restored, forests are managed sustainably, desertification is defied, land degradation is reversed and halted, and diversity loss is prevented.
